2. Use of Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our services.
  • To personalize your experience on our platform.
  • To connect recruiters and talents in a professional network.
  • To import your LinkedIn, Google, or Zoom profile, when you authorize it via OAuth.

  • To communicate with you, including sending notifications and updates about our services.
  • To ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations.
  • To protect against fraud, unauthorized activities, and illegal activities.

3. LinkedIn, Google, and Zoom OAuth Login

If you choose to connect your LinkedIn, Google, or Zoom account using OAuth 2.0, we will request permission to access certain information from your respective profile. This may include your name, profile picture, email address, job title, education, certifications, and other professional information.

How We Use Your OAuth Data:

  • Your LinkedIn and Google data will be used to populate your profile on our platform, making it easier for you to connect with recruiters and present your professional credentials.
  • Your Zoom data will only be used for scheduling meetings. We do not use your Zoom account for any other purpose.
  • We will not store or use your LinkedIn, Google, or Zoom login credentials.
  • You have control over the data shared and can disconnect your LinkedIn, Google, or Zoom account at any time.
